# How to Connect a Bluetooth Printer

## Prerequisites
- Printer powered on and in pairing mode
- Relay app installed and connected to server
- Bluetooth enabled on Android device

## Enable Printer Pairing Mode
1. Turn on your thermal printer
2. Press and hold the pairing/feed button until LED blinks rapidly
3. Printer is now discoverable

## Pair in Android Settings
1. Open Android **Settings**
2. Tap **Bluetooth**
3. Ensure Bluetooth is ON
4. Tap **Pair New Device** or **Scan**
5. Wait for your printer to appear in the list
6. Tap the printer name
7. Confirm pairing (PIN usually 0000 or 1234 if prompted)
8. Wait for "Paired" status

## Select Printer in Relay App
1. Open the Relay app
2. Tap the **Printer** icon
3. Tap **Scan for Printers**
4. Select your paired printer from the list
5. Tap **Connect**
6. Wait for connection confirmation

## Test the Connection
1. Tap **Test Print** button in the app
2. Printer should print a test receipt showing:
   - Current date/time
   - Device info
   - Connection status
3. Verify print quality is clear and readable

## Expected Outcome
Printer status shows "Connected" and a green indicator. The app is ready to receive and print orders.

## Troubleshooting
- **Printer not found:** Ensure printer is in pairing mode and Bluetooth is enabled
- **Pairing failed:** Try turning printer off and on, then pair again
- **Connection drops:** Keep printer within 10 meters of Android device
- **Print quality poor:** Check paper quality and printer head cleanliness
