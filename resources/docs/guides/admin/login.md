# How to Log In

## Steps
1. Open your web browser and navigate to the admin dashboard URL
2. Enter your username in the "Username" field
3. Enter your password in the "Password" field
4. Click the "Sign In" button

## Expected Outcome
Upon successful login, you will be redirected to the dashboard home page where you can see order statistics and quick actions.

## Troubleshooting
- **Incorrect credentials:** Verify your username and password
- **Account locked:** Contact your administrator
- **Page not loading:** Check your internet connection
