Change 03 / 05
Card — border, blur & shadow
● High impact
The card border was border-white/65 — invisible against light backgrounds. Switch to the system card border, lighten the blur to match Step 2, and bring the shadow into proportion (plus a dark-mode shadow).
resources/js/components/ui/card/Card.vue
// Root card class
- border-white/65
+ border-black/8                                  // visible in light mode
- backdrop-blur-xl
+ backdrop-blur-sm
- shadow-[0_28px_60px_-38px_rgba(37,37,37,0.38)]
+ shadow-[0_24px_55px_-36px_rgba(37,37,37,0.32)]
+ dark:shadow-[0_24px_55px_-36px_rgba(0,0,0,0.38)]
Before → after border
border-white/65→border-black/8
backdrop-blur-xl→backdrop-blur-sm
Why system-wide

border-black/8 is the same stroke used on the Dashboard and Devices cards in Step 4 — theming Card.vue here means those pages line up automatically.
